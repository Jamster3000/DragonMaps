Licensing for Maps Created with DragonMaps

This still applies for anything required using the above assets or other digital material.

  1. Attribution: Users must give credit to DragonMaps when sharing or distributing maps created with our tool.
  2. Dual Licensing: Maps created with DragonMaps fall under two categories:
    • Maps using only DragonMaps-provided assets: These must be used for non-commercial purposes only, in line with our asset providers' terms.
    • Maps incorporating user's own assets or copyright free assets: These can be used for both commercial and non-commercial purposes, subject to attribution.
  3. Freedom to Share: Users are free to share, copy, and redistribute maps they create in any medium or format.
  4. Freedom to Adapt: Users can remix, transform, and build upon their maps for any purpose, even commercially if using their own assets or other appropriate copyright licenses.

This license is inspired by the Creative Commons Attribution (CC BY) license, modified to fit the specific requirements of our asset providers and to accommodate user-owned assets.


In practice, this means you can:

  • Use maps created with DragonMaps for personal or commercial gaming sessions, or other use, depending on the assets used.
  • Share maps you've created with other users or on community platforms, providing proper attribution to DragonMaps.
  • Modify or build upon maps created with DragonMaps.
  • Use maps commercially if they incorporate your own assets, as long as you provide attribution to DragonMaps for the tool.

You must:

  • Provide attribution to DragonMaps when sharing or distributing maps created with our tool.
